Marchpane.

Ingredients:
* Two bags of fresh almonds
* One pinch of white sugar

Instructions:
* Grind almonds in mortar until fine paste
* Add sugar to paste and mix
* Arrange with almonds at corners and sugar above
* Craft recipe

Serves 1.
