A beast of South,
it drills and drolls;
it guards the pass
but lets none pass.
It digs and kicks
through rock and sand;
it chews the soil,
but eats no salt.
No Men can stay
the lengthy might;
no Men can stop
the endless search:
in search of food,
and joyous fun.
